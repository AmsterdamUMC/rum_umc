# Wednesday, June 19th, 2024. Session 1: tidytuesday
First session to kick-off the group & a short demonstration of tidytuesday, by [Sam Langton](https://www.samlangton.info) ([Amsterdam UMC](https://www.amsterdamumc.org/en.htm))

## Introduction
In this session, we kick-off the R user meeting for Amsterdam UMC (RUM-UMC) with personal introductions, a conversation to find out people's preferences for format and timings (e.g., frequency of meets, hybrid), followed by a brief demonstration of [tidytuesday](https://github.com/rfordatascience/tidytuesday).
